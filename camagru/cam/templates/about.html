About Camagru

What is Camagru?

Camagru is a social media platform that allows users to take pictures and share them with their friends. The platform was created in 2024 by eyagiz, a software developer based in the Turkey. The idea for Camagru came from eyagiz's school ÃŠcole 42, where he was inspired by the idea of creating a social media platform that focused on photos and visual content.

How does Camagru work?

Camagru works by allowing users to take pictures using their smartphone or webcam and then share them with their friends. Users can add filters, stickers, and text to their photos to make them more fun and engaging. Users can also like and comment on each other's photos, and share them on other social media platforms like Facebook and Twitter.
